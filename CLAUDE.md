# Workshop Feb 26 - New Team Workshop

## Project Purpose

Plan, track, and build context for the first workshop of our new team. This repository serves as the central coordination point for all workshop logistics, content, and follow-up.

## Workshop Overview

- **Event**: First team workshop
- **Date**: February 26 (week-long, 5 days)
- **Status**: Planning phase

## Agenda

### Day 1 — Mission, Goals & Way of Working
- Team mission and vision alignment
- Goals for the quarter and beyond
- Way of working: practices, rituals, and tools
- Topics: use cases, experiments, customers, and more

### Day 2 — Becoming AI First Developers
- What it means to be an AI First team
- Tools, workflows, and mindset shifts
- Hands-on exercises with AI-assisted development

### Day 3 — Deep Dive: Main Initiatives
Get everyone up to speed on the 3 main initiatives:
- **ExCat** — overview, current status, and roadmap
- **Business Insights** — overview, current status, and roadmap
- **Generative Websites** — overview, current status, and roadmap

### Day 4 & 5 — AI First Hackathon
- Form teams and pick challenges
- Build, iterate, and demo
- Judging and retrospective

## Key Areas

### Logistics
